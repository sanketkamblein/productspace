The Challenge

Two Critical Gaps in Industrial Operations

Yokogawa's customers — heavy industry manufacturers — were operating without the tools needed to act on their own sensor data, and had no visibility into how energy consumption translated to emissions.

01

No Actionable Sensor Intelligence

Heavy industry manufacturers lacked efficient, user-friendly tools to analyse sensor data from equipment — making it impossible to optimise production, prevent downtime, or make informed maintenance decisions.

Temperature Pressure Vibration Energy Meter
02

Blind Spot on Carbon Footprint

No comprehensive dashboard existed to correlate electricity consumption data from multiple sources with CO2 emissions — leaving Sustainability Managers without the data needed to meet emissions targets.

The Solution

EcoPulse: One Platform, Two Superpowers

A user-friendly bundle of applications that bridges Yokogawa's sensor hardware with SAP's enterprise backbone — giving plant operators real-time equipment intelligence and sustainability managers clear visibility into carbon footprint.

🔌

Data Integration

Sensors transmit to Yokogawa's system; Yokogawa pushes data to EcoPulse via file upload or direct API — flexible for any plant topology.

🗺️

Sensor & Equipment Mapping

View and manage which sensors are mapped to which equipment across the plant floor. Full master data visibility in one place.

📊

Real-Time Dashboard

Equipment health and live sensor readings at a specific point in time — giving Plant Managers the clarity to act fast.

⚙️

Business Rules Engine

Predefined conditions auto-trigger maintenance notifications and planned maintenance orders in S/4 HANA — from alert to action without manual effort.

🌡️

Special Sensor Support

Certain sensors emit multiple data types from a single device. EcoPulse's data model handles multi-datapoint sensors natively.

🌱

Carbon Footprint Tracker

Unified electricity usage across all sources, correlated with CO2 emissions and configurable per local regulations.

User Research

Three Personas, One Coherent Experience

I mapped the distinct jobs-to-be-done across the three core users of EcoPulse and designed the product flows around their specific decision-making needs.

♻️

Sustainability Manager

  • Analyze carbon footprint across the plant
  • Take informed steps to reduce CO2 emissions
  • Monitor electricity-to-emissions correlation
🏭

Plant Manager

  • Monitor health of all equipment in real time
  • Receive alerts when thresholds are breached
  • Make informed maintenance decisions
🔧

Admin

  • Configure CO2 calculations per local regulations
  • Maintain the configuration master with Plant Manager
  • Set up and manage business rules as needed

What I Did

Product Design Contributions

My Specific Contributions

Problem Framing: Translated Yokogawa's customer pain points into two clear, testable problem statements that anchored the entire team's direction.

Persona Design: Defined three distinct user personas — Sustainability Manager, Plant Manager, and Admin — each with specific jobs-to-be-done and system responsibilities.

Screen Flow Architecture: Designed the full screen flows for the Dashboard view (Plant Manager) and Admin configuration view, establishing the navigation and interaction model.

Feature Definition: Authored the feature set — Data Integration, Sensor Mapping, Dashboard, Business Rules, Special Sensor support, Carbon Tracker — with clear scope for development.

Presentation Design: Created and structured the Concept → Design → Build presentation deck delivered to judges at Invent for Customer 2023.

Product Vision: Synthesised the dual-value proposition — operational intelligence + sustainability tracking — into a coherent product story that resonated with Yokogawa stakeholders.
