About Me

Hi — I'm Sanket. Before I was a product manager or a designer, I was a painter, a musician, and a software developer. I have always been drawn to the edges where disciplines meet and bleed into each other.

I paint and sketch — mostly human portraits. When I draw someone, I am not just observing their features. I am trying to understand what they are feeling, what they are holding back. That practice of deep empathy is inseparable from how I do design and product work.

Music has been with me since school — performing, not just listening. Both painting and music taught me that there is a language beyond words, one that connects directly to how people think and feel without being spoken aloud. I look for that language in everything I build.

At IISc Bangalore, studying M.Des opened up something I hadn't expected — I became a researcher. I started seeing the hidden threads connecting art, science, and design. That curiosity hasn't left me. I am still following it.

Professionally, I have 8+ years across ABAP development at SAP, UX design, and early-stage product management — working on Supply Chain ERP, Sustainability, Healthcare, MarTech, and AI-powered tools. I thrive in 0–1 spaces where the problem isn't defined yet.
